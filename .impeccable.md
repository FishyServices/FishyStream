## Design Context

### Users
FishyStream is for people browsing and watching movies or TV on phones, laptops, and desktop monitors. They need to discover content quickly, jump back into active shows, and start playback with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
Confident, cinematic, fast. The interface should feel polished and immersive without becoming heavy or cluttered.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark streaming-first UI with bold contrast, crisp type, and compact controls that stay usable on touch screens. It should feel premium and focused, not like a Netflix clone and not like a generic SaaS dashboard.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize playback and discovery over decorative chrome.
2. Preserve all core actions on mobile; adapt them instead of hiding them behind hover.
3. Keep layouts fluid from narrow phones to large desktops with strong spacing rhythm.
4. Prefer touch-friendly controls and readable metadata on small screens.
5. Maintain the cinematic tone with restrained motion and high-contrast hierarchy.
